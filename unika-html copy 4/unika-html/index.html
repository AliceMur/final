Target Audience

High school & college students

The Problem

We discovered that finding a collective list of specific volunteer opportunities in the tri-state area is difficult because many of them remain unreported.

Our Solution

This easy-to-use website allows organizations to add volunteer/donation opportunities according to their respective category. Volunteers who sign in may also log their service hours.

Why

As fellow high school students who are looking, this website can be incredibly useful for other students looking to give back to their community.
